# Platform Policy Document

## AI-Powered Parametric Insurance Platform for Gig Economy Workers

This document defines the **operational policies, coverage rules, and governance framework** for the AI-powered parametric insurance platform built for gig economy delivery workers.

The goal of this platform is to provide **automated income protection** for workers who lose working hours due to external disruptions such as extreme weather, pollution, curfews, or delivery platform outages.

---

# 1. Policy Objective

The objective of this platform is to:

* Protect gig workers from **income loss caused by external disruptions**
* Provide **automated insurance coverage**
* Enable **instant claim processing**
* Reduce fraud through **AI-based verification**
* Deliver **transparent and explainable insurance decisions**

This platform operates using a **parametric insurance model**, where claims are triggered automatically when predefined conditions are met.

---

# 2. Coverage Scope Policy

The platform provides coverage **only for loss of income caused by external disruptions**.

## Covered Disruptions

The following disruption categories may trigger insurance payouts:

### Environmental Disruptions

* Extreme heatwaves
* Heavy rainfall
* Flooding
* Severe air pollution
* Extreme cold conditions

### Social Disruptions

* Government curfews
* Local strikes
* Market or zone closures
* Road blockades

### Platform Disruptions

* Delivery platform outages
* API failures
* App downtime affecting deliveries

Coverage applies only when **disruptions prevent workers from performing deliveries**.

---

# 3. Excluded Coverage

The platform **does not provide traditional insurance coverage**.

The following categories are strictly excluded:

* Health insurance
* Life insurance
* Accident insurance
* Vehicle damage or repairs
* Medical expenses
* Personal injuries

The platform is designed solely for **income loss protection**.

---

# 4. Worker Eligibility Policy

To register on the platform, users must:

1. Be an active gig delivery worker.
2. Be associated with a recognized delivery platform.
3. Provide accurate onboarding information.

Required registration details include:

* Name
* Phone number
* Delivery platform
* Work city
* Delivery zone

The system generates a **risk profile and trust score** for each worker after registration.

---

# 5. Weekly Insurance Policy Rules

All insurance policies operate on a **weekly subscription model**.

This structure aligns with the **typical earning cycle of gig workers**.

Example plans include:

| Plan     | Weekly Premium | Coverage |
| -------- | -------------- | -------- |
| Basic    | ₹5             | ₹300     |
| Standard | ₹8             | ₹500     |
| Premium  | ₹12            | ₹800     |

Policies:

* Activate after premium payment
* Automatically renew every week
* Expire if payment is not completed

Workers may upgrade or downgrade plans before the next renewal cycle.

---

# 6. Parametric Trigger Policy

Insurance payouts are triggered when **predefined disruption thresholds are exceeded**.

Example triggers include:

| Event            | Trigger Condition                |
| ---------------- | -------------------------------- |
| Heatwave         | Temperature > 48°C               |
| Extreme Cold     | Temperature < 10°C               |
| Heavy Rain       | Rainfall above defined threshold |
| Flood Alert      | Water level warning issued       |
| Pollution Event  | AQI > 400                        |
| Curfew           | Verified government announcement |
| Platform Failure | Delivery platform API outage     |

When a trigger condition is detected, the platform automatically initiates the claim process.

---

# 7. Automated Claim Processing Policy

Claims are processed automatically using system data and AI validation.

The claim verification process checks:

* Worker location
* Active insurance policy
* Event authenticity
* Estimated income loss

Once validated, the platform initiates an **instant payout**.

Workers do not need to manually file claims.

---

# 8. AI Risk Intelligence Policy

The platform uses AI models to assess disruption risks and estimate income loss.

AI systems analyze:

* Weather data
* Air pollution levels
* Traffic conditions
* Historical disruption patterns
* Worker activity data

Based on this analysis, the platform calculates:

* Risk score
* Premium adjustments
* Expected disruption probability

---

# 9. Fraud Prevention Policy

To maintain platform integrity, the system includes multiple fraud detection mechanisms.

These include:

* GPS spoofing detection
* Location verification
* Duplicate claim detection
* Abnormal activity monitoring
* Crowd-based event validation

Accounts involved in fraudulent activities may face:

* Claim rejection
* Temporary suspension
* Permanent account termination

---

# 10. Data Privacy and Security Policy

The platform protects user data using secure infrastructure and authentication mechanisms.

Data collected includes:

* User identity information
* Location data
* Policy records
* Claim history

Security practices include:

* Encrypted data storage
* Token-based authentication
* Secure API communication
* Access control mechanisms

User data will not be shared with third parties without consent except where necessary for platform operations.

# 11. Transparency and Explainability Policy

The platform follows **Explainable AI principles**.

Users can view:

* Why a disruption event was detected
* Which parametric trigger was activated
* How compensation was calculated

This ensures fairness and transparency in automated decisions.

---

# 12. Payout Policy

All approved claims result in **instant payout processing**.

Payments may be processed through:

* UPI simulation
* Razorpay sandbox
* Stripe test environment

Payout amount is calculated based on:

* Estimated lost work hours
* Selected coverage plan
* Policy limits
